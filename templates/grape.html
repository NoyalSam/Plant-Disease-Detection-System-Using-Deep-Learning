How It Works

This tool uses a deep learning model trained to identify grape diseases from images. Simply upload a clear photo of a grape leaf, and our AI will analyze it to determine if it's healthy or identify potential diseases.

Detectable Diseases

  • Black Rot - Characterized by brown circular lesions
  • Esca (Black Measles) - Shows reddish-brown patches between veins
  • Leaf Blight (Isariopsis Leaf Spot) - Displays small, dark spots
  • Healthy - Normal green leaf tissue

For Best Results

  • Use well-lit, clear images
  • Focus on the affected area of the leaf
  • Avoid shadows or glare
  • Use high-resolution images when possible
